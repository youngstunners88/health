---
name: payment-agent
description: >
  Unified payment‑processing skill for the health‑care swarm with
  **dynamic staking**, **tiered‑reward multipliers**, and **sub‑pool splitting**.
  Use when Kofi needs to:
  (1) Send/receive cUSD/cEUR on the Celo blockchain,
  (2) Charge/refund traditional cards (Visa/MasterCard) or mobile wallets
      (Apple Pay, Google Pay),
  (3) Verify transaction status,
  (4) Deposit a **risk‑adjusted fraction** of each successful payment into a
      chosen sub‑pool of the staking contract,
  (5) Apply a **tiered‑reward multiplier** based on the patient’s claim‑free
      streak,
  (6) Emit a human‑readable receipt via the humanizer skill.
